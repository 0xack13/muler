Muler does NOT provide prescribing or dispensing advice.

Muler is a concise pharmacological reference for students of healthcare professions. The BNF, Medscape, and MedicinesComplete are professional resources containing detailed, up-to-date information required for prescribing and dispensing—and you should totally use them! However, in my experience at least, they aren't the most useful for learning and consolidating pharmacology knowledge, especially when sometimes what you need is a quick reminder of how pilocarpine works.

Rather than replacing existing educational resources, Muler is a handy little search engine that lets you quickly look up the pharmacodynamics and mechanism of action of 13,579 drugs. This information comes a dataset by DrugBank, and it is boiled down to the relevant bits: common indications, mechanism of action, and pharmacodynamics.
